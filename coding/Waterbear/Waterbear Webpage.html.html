What are the "water bears"?


Water bears are some tiny creature that live in water. They are so tiny so we can only see them with a microscope.Something amazing about them is they almost can survive in any kind of environment!

They can live many days without water or oxygen! Even x-rays, microwave, and acid liquid can't kill them at short time. How do water bears do this? By swiching to "hide-living" mode!
