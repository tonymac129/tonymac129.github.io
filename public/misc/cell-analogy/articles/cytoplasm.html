Cytoplasm: Motherboard

If you compare cell organells to different components of a laptop according to their functions, a cell's cytoplasm would be the motherboard of the laptop. The main functions of cytoplasm are holding the cell parts and organells together, transporting nutrients, and keeping the cell shape. Cytoplasm is a jelly-like substance made from water, salt, and other stuff. A laptop's motherboard is also really like the cytoplasm, because it holds all the components of a laptop like CPU, chips, wires, battery, disks, fans, and more. If there wan't a motherboard all the components would be messed up and not working at all, same with the cytoplasm. So in conclusion, cytoplasm and motherboard are similar because they both keep the parts together so they can work with each other without a problem.
