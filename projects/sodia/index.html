Welcome to Sodia!

Sodia is a social media platform where users can share their thoughts, experiences, and ideas and genuinely connect with other people. Obviously this is a mock up platform and isn't a real social media. It's just my personal design project showcasing my ideal social media where there's no ads, no bots, and just pure content and people connecting with each other in diferent ways through posts, clips, or chat messages. I really like Reddit's approach to post interactions and Discord's messenging features/layout, so I definitely took a lot of inspiration from those. I also blended some TikTok/YouTube/X design elements into Sodia.
