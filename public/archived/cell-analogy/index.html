What if a laptop was a cell?

By Tony, Period 3

Nucleus: CPU

The main functions of a nucleus inside a cell are to store the DNA for reproduction and to control the parts, or organnels, inside the cell. Because laptops don't need to reproduce, the nucleus of the laptop is the component that controls the whole laptop, which is the CPU, or central processing unit. It controls the whole laptop by getting the user input, processing the information logically, and sending out the processed information to different parts of the laptop.

Cell Membrane: Ports

What the cell membrane does in a cell is that it controls what enters and what leaves the cell. Some of the files and programs of a laptop are created internally or are downloaded from the web, and the operating system controls what are downloaded and what aren't. Ports on the sides of the laptop controls what are connected to the laptop and what are connected through the ports.

Cytoplasm: Motherboard

Cytoplasm holds all the organnels and things in a cell together and keeps the shape of a cell. Motherboard does the exact same thing in a laptop. It holds all the components, wires, and structures of a laptop together on a piece of specially designed metal board.

Ribosomes: Keyboard and Mouse

What ribosomes in a cell do is that they produce proteins that determine the cell's function, or tell a cell what to do. When you're using a laptop, you use the mouse and the keyboard to click, select, type, etc. , or producing the commands that tell the laptop what to do, which is exactly like what ribosomes do.

Mitochondria: Battery

Mitochondia are the powerhouses for a cell. They turn glucose, or nutrients that come from food, into energy for the cell. Battery in a laptop, on the other hand, turns the chemical energy inside the battery into electricity for the laptop to use by releasing the electrons of the battery.

Vacuoles: Fans

Vacuoles store water and other nutrients for the cell and get rid of waste products from the cell. Because all the nutrients, or things, needed for laptop is just electricity, and battery is already handling that. Heat is pretty much all the waste product a laptop produces, and laptops get rid of heat by using fast turning fans and a cooling pad.

Chloroplasts: Power Port

What chloroplasts do is that they absorb sunlight's radient energy and use that energy to break and merge carbon dioxide and oxygen together into glucose for mitochondria to use. A power port works the same way, it absorbs the power from the charger, and turns that energy into chemical energy for the battery to utillize.
