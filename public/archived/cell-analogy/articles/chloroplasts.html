Chloroplasts: Power Port

If you compare cell organells to different components of a laptop according to their functions, a cell's chloroplasts would be the power port of the laptop. The main function of chloroplasts is executing photosynthesis. They first absorb the radient energy from the sunlight, then use that energy to move around the electrons in the carbon dioxide and the water, which would result in a glucose compund and oxygen. The glucose would be later used by the mitochondria. The power port, on the other hand, leads the electricity from the charger into the battery, which is similar to mitochondria. Like chloroplasts give mitochondria glucose to use, the power port gives the battery energy. This is what makes them similar to each other.
