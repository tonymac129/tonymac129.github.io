Mitochondria: Battery

If you compare cell organells to different components of a laptop according to their functions, a cell's mitochondria would be the battery of the laptop. The main function of mitochondria is that they produce energy that the cell can use by breaking down the chemical compounds in food called glucose. They break down glucose into carbon dioxide, water, and energy for the cell to use. Battery is pretty similar to mitochondria because it also converts the chemical potential energy to electromagnetic energy so the laptop will have electricity (energy) to run. Bettery turns the charged chemical energy into electricity for the laptop by releasing the electrons in it. Both mitochondria and battery turn a form of energy into another which their parent element can use to do things. their parent element what to do.
