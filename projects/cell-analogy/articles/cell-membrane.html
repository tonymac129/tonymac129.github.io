Cell Membrane: Ports

If you compare cell organells to different components of a laptop according to their functions, a cell's cell membrane would be the ports of the laptop. The main function of a cell membrane is controlling what enters and leaves the cell. It keeps harmful stuff from the cell and makes nutrients, important chemical compounds, and oxygen be able to get in the cell. Laptop ports, on the other hand, also control what enters and what leaves the laptop. It makes it possible to connect devices to the laptop but keeps other stuff like pencils, paper, etc. out of the laptop. It also controls whether or not if pieces of information and files can exit the laptop. In conclusion, both cell membrane and laptop ports are very similar because they both control what enters and leaves their parent element.
