Vacuoles: Fans

If you compare cell organells to different components of a laptop according to their functions, a cell's vacuoles would be the fans of the laptop. The main functions of vacuoles are storing water and nutrients for the cell and getting rid of the waste product. They break the large chemical compounds into smaller smaller molecules and send them out of the cell. The fans in a laptop and the cooling pad do the same thing, too. They get rid of the heat, or waste, created by the laptop by fanning, blowing, and directing the heat out of the laptop. To summarize, cell vacuoles and laptop fans are similar because they both get rid of the waste product produced by their parent element.
